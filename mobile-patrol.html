ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Do your mobile patrols cover my area in Nigeria?

Yes! Our mobile patrol teams operate across Lagos (Mainland and Island), Abuja FCT, Port Harcourt, Ibadan, Kano, Enugu, and all major Nigerian cities. We deploy patrol vehicles 24/7 and can cover multiple client locations on scheduled routes. Whether your property is in highbrow areas or industrial zones, our branded patrol vans provide visible deterrence and rapid response throughout your locality.

How many times will you patrol my property daily?

We customize patrol frequencies based on your needs and risk level. Standard packages include 2-4 daily patrols (morning, afternoon, evening, night), while premium packages offer hourly or every-two-hours checks. Weekend and overnight patrols are available. Each visit includes perimeter checks, alarm testing, and documented reports. We provide flexible scheduling that fits your security requirements and budget.

What happens if your patrol team discovers a security issue?

Our patrol guards immediately alert you via phone call, WhatsApp, or SMS with photos/videos of the issue. They secure the area, contact our control room for backup, and if necessary, coordinate with police. For break-in attempts, fire, or flooding, we take immediate action to minimize damage. You receive detailed incident reports with timestamps and evidence for insurance or legal purposes.

Can I track your patrol visits to verify you came?

Absolutely! We provide daily patrol reports with exact visit times, photos of your premises, and any observations noted. Our patrol vehicles use GPS tracking, and clients can request timestamped proof of each visit. We maintain transparent operations because we understand trust is essential. Monthly summary reports are also provided showing all patrol activities at your property.

Do your patrols operate during bad weather and heavy traffic?

Yes, our mobile patrols operate in all weather conditions including rainy season downpours. We understand Lagos traffic and Nigerian road challenges, so we schedule patrols accounting for peak traffic hours. Our drivers are experienced navigating flooded roads, potholes, and congested areas. Your security coverage continues uninterrupted regardless of weather or traffic conditions.

What payment options are available for mobile patrol services?

We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual payment plans with discounts for longer commitments. Payment via bank transfer to our Nigerian accounts (all major banks), mobile money transfers, or corporate cheques are accepted. For businesses, we provide monthly invoicing with flexible terms. Weekly payment arrangements are also available for short-term patrol needs during special periods or vacations.
