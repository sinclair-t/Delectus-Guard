ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

What types of events do you provide security for across Nigeria?

We secure all event types including weddings, corporate conferences, product launches, concerts, political rallies, religious gatherings, award ceremonies, sports tournaments, festivals, and private parties. We've successfully secured major events at Eko Hotels, Transcorp Hilton Abuja, Landmark Event Centre, and outdoor venues across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and all Nigerian cities.

How early should I book your event security services?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks ahead for proper planning and personnel allocation, especially for large events or peak periods like December owambe season. However, we understand Nigerian party culture and can mobilize emergency security teams within 48-72 hours for urgent events. Early booking allows us to conduct site assessments and develop comprehensive security plans.

Do you handle crowd control for large Nigerian events?

Yes, we specialize in crowd management for events with thousands of attendees. Our guards are trained to handle Nigerian crowd dynamics professionally and respectfully. We manage entry points, control queues, prevent gate-crashing, handle VIP sections, coordinate with event bouncers, and maintain order while ensuring your guests feel welcomed and safe throughout the event.

What payment options do you offer for event security?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (GTBank, UBA, Zenith, etc.), and for established clients, we offer payment plans with 50% upfront and balance after the event. For corporate events, we provide invoicing with flexible terms. Payment can be made days before your event, and we issue proper receipts for your records and accounting purposes.

Can you provide security for outdoor events during rainy season?

Absolutely! Our guards come prepared with rain gear and maintain full security coverage regardless of weather conditions. We adapt security positions based on rain, coordinate with event organizers on crowd movement during downpours, and ensure parking areas and entry points remain secured. Nigerian weather won't compromise your event security - we're prepared for all conditions.

Do you coordinate with police for high-profile events?

Yes, for events with government officials, celebrities, or large crowds, we coordinate with local police divisions and obtain necessary permits. We maintain excellent relationships with police commands across Nigeria and can facilitate official police presence when required. Our team works seamlessly with law enforcement to ensure comprehensive security coverage for your high-profile event.
