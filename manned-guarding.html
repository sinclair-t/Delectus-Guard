Professional Manned Guarding Services Across Nigeria

Letters Guard Security Services provides highly trained, professional security guards for businesses, residential estates, industrial facilities, and institutions throughout Nigeria. Our manned guarding solutions deliver visible deterrence, access control, and immediate response to security incidents 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

With a rigorous recruitment and training process, our security personnel are equipped to handle diverse security challenges unique to Nigeria's operational environment. From corporate headquarters in Lagos to manufacturing facilities in Port Harcourt, our guards maintain the highest standards of professionalism, vigilance, and customer service.

Our Manned Guarding Solutions Include:

  • Static security guards for premises protection
  • Access control and visitor management
  • 24/7 security coverage with rotating shifts
  • CCTV monitoring and surveillance support
  • Incident reporting and documentation
  • Emergency response and crisis management
  • Patrol and perimeter security checks
  • Fire safety and evacuation assistance
