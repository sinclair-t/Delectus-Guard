VIP & EXECUTIVE PROTECTION SERVICES

At Letters Guard, we provide elite close protection services for VIPs, executives, dignitaries, celebrities, and high-profile individuals across Nigeria. Our highly trained protection officers combine discretion with vigilance, ensuring your personal safety while maintaining your privacy and freedom of movement. Whether you require protection for business travel, public appearances, or daily activities, our team delivers professional, unobtrusive security tailored to your specific needs and threat level.

Our executive protection specialists are former military, law enforcement, and intelligence professionals with extensive experience in threat assessment, defensive tactics, emergency response, and close quarters protection. We conduct comprehensive security assessments, develop detailed protection plans, and coordinate all aspects of your security - from advance route reconnaissance to secure transportation and residential security. With Letters Guard, you can focus on your responsibilities while we ensure your safety across Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and throughout Nigeria.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

What qualifications do your close protection officers have?

Our close protection officers are former military personnel, law enforcement professionals, and trained security specialists with extensive backgrounds in defensive tactics, threat assessment, emergency response, and VIP protection. All officers undergo rigorous screening, continuous training, and hold relevant certifications in executive protection and crisis management.

How discreet is your VIP protection service?

Discretion is paramount in our executive protection services. Our officers are trained to blend seamlessly into any environment - from corporate settings to social events - providing vigilant security without drawing attention. We maintain your privacy while ensuring comprehensive protection at all times.

Can you provide protection for international travel?

Yes. We coordinate international protection services through our network of trusted security partners worldwide. We conduct advance security assessments, arrange secure transportation, coordinate with local authorities, and provide continuous protection throughout your international travels.

What is included in your threat risk assessment?

Our comprehensive threat assessments analyze your specific risk profile, daily routines, travel patterns, public exposure, and potential vulnerabilities. We identify credible threats, assess your security needs, and develop customized protection strategies including secure routes, safe locations, and emergency protocols tailored to your lifestyle and requirements.
